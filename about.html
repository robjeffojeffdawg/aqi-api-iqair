🌍 About Live AQI Monitor

Live AQI Monitor is a real-time air quality tracking application that helps you understand the air you breathe, wherever you are in the world.

What is AQI?

The Air Quality Index (AQI) is a standardized indicator of air pollution levels. It tells you how clean or polluted your air is, and what health effects might be a concern. Higher numbers mean greater health risk.

0-50: Good ✓ Safe for everyone
51-100: Moderate ⚠️ Acceptable for most
101-150: Unhealthy for Sensitive ⚠️ Limit outdoor activity
151-200: Unhealthy 🚨 Everyone affected
201-300: Very Unhealthy 🚨 Health alert
301+: Hazardous ☠️ Emergency conditions

How It Works

This app uses your device's location to find the nearest air quality monitoring station and displays real-time pollution data including PM2.5, PM10, ozone, and other pollutants. Data is provided by IQAir and PurpleAir, combining professional monitoring stations with community sensors for comprehensive coverage.

Data Sources

IQAir: Professional air quality monitoring stations operated by governments and environmental agencies worldwide.

PurpleAir: Community-operated sensors providing hyperlocal, real-time PM2.5 data with high sensor density in urban areas.

Privacy

Your location is only used to fetch nearby air quality data. No personal information is stored or transmitted to any third parties. Location access is requested only when you click "Use My Location" and is never saved.
