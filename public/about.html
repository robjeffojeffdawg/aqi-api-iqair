Live AQI Monitor is a real-time air quality tracking application that helps you understand the air you breathe, wherever you are in the world.

What is AQI?

The Air Quality Index (AQI) is a standardized indicator of air pollution levels. It tells you how clean or polluted your air is, and what health effects might be a concern. Higher numbers mean greater health risk.

How It Works

This app uses your device's location to find the nearest air quality monitoring station and displays real-time pollution data including PM2.5, PM10, ozone, and other pollutants. Data is provided by IQAir, one of the world's leading air quality information platforms.

Privacy

Your location is only used to fetch nearby air quality data. No personal information is stored or transmitted to any third parties. Location access is requested only when you click "Use My Location" and is never saved.
