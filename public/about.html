Live AQI Monitor is a real-time air quality application that helps you understand the air you breathe ‚Äî anywhere in the world.

The app provides Air Quality Index (AQI) values, pollutant data, and weather conditions using IQAir, one of the world's leading air quality data platforms.

AQI values are presented using internationally recognized standards (US AQI and China AQI), along with health guidance to help you make informed decisions about outdoor activities.

What is AQI?

The Air Quality Index (AQI) is a number used to communicate how polluted the air is. Higher numbers mean more pollution and greater health concerns.

Privacy

This project was built with privacy in mind. No personal data is stored, and location access is used only to fetch nearby air quality data when explicitly allowed by you.
